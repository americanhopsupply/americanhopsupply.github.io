Welcome to AHS

Direct From The Source
American Hop Supply provides the finest quality hops from the Pacific Northwest and is located in the heart of the Yakima Valley. Home of the largest hop production in the United States.
We are here to provide a wide variety of hops direct from local farms to brewers around the world.

Quality and Care
From farm to table, or farm to pint we can provide detailed information on the source of your hops.

The Bold and the Bitterfull

We collect over 30 varieties of hops direct from the growers. Whether it is seasonal fresh hops, whole leaf or pellets. American Hop Supply is the source for brewers around the world.

Mission

Our goal is to provide excellent customer service along with the freshest, highest quality hops to beer enthusiasts around the world
