About AHS

American Hop Supply is located in Moxee, Washington and is surrounded by thousands of acres of hop fields.

Moxee is part of the Yakima Valley which grows over 77 percent of the United States hop crop. This allows AHS to source the freshest hops available. No long-haul trucking for processing or storage. Our hops go directly from the field to the drying rooms, take a short cooling off rest, then go promptly into controlled storage environments to maximize freshness and quality.

AHS collect hops directly from 3rd and 4th generation hop farms and distribute them to brewers worldwide. We are also uniquely positioned to provide hops from other spot market sources, which allows us to offer a broad range of American and International varieties to consolidate orders to help save customers freight costs. 

Our goal is to provide excellent customer service along with the freshest, highest quality hops to beer enthusiasts
around the world

About Mike

Mike McCrea is the founder of American Hop Supply. His background includes over 25 years of international business in the agriculture industry. Whether it involved expanding existing markets or opening new ones Mike has traveled extensively throughout 18 different countries. In addition to direct customer contacts Mike’s promotional and advertising efforts have included celebrity product endorsements, global trade and consumer media buys, social media promotions and even placement of a billboard in Times Square, NYC.

Marrying into a hop farming family and later moving to Moxee, Washington, Mike began to take a stronger interest in the hop industry. After over a decade of global research in hop sales and countless discussions with local hop producers, Mike began to narrow his agriculture focus exclusively on hop sales and marketing. This led to the founding of American Hop Supply.

“I increasingly found the hop producers, dealers and brewers all enjoy their trade and I admired the innovative efforts involved to produce great new products. It’s hard not to be attracted to an industry where everyone truly enjoys doing what they do.”  - Mike.

American Hop Supply is dedicated to finding hop solutions for brewers throughout the United States and assist the growing global demand for American style hops to the international craft brewing industry.
